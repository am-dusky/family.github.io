Types of Family

Nuclear Family

Nuclear families, also known as elementary or traditional families, consist of two parents (usually married or common law) and their children. .

Single Parent

Single parent families consist of one parent with one or more kids. In these cases, the parent either never married, is widowed, or divorced

Extended Family

Extended families are families with two or more adults who are related through blood or marriage, usually along with children.
